step one

making white cross

first :making flower
you should make flower around yellow center with white edges
some cases
second: matching it with white center
finally you got it and make first step

step two

making white corners

first : the white corner should be between his centers by (R)
corner which contains red,blue should be between blue corner,red corner
in some cases the white corner where is in bottom layer and shouldn't be in his centers
soluation: (R U R’ U’) one time then it will be in top layer and move it by (R) to be in his centers

positions

first position
alogrthim: (R U R’ U’) one Time
second position
alogrthim: (R U R’ U’) two Time
third position
alogrthim: (R U R’ U’) three Time
fourth position
alogrthim: (R U R’ U’) four Time
fifth position
alogrthim: (U R U’ R’) one Time
finally you got it and made second step

step three

making edges

there is two cases in this step
in this step you should find edge which dosen't in include yellow color and move it by (R) to be between his centers
first case
in this case if the top of edge's center in the right
alogrthim: U (R U R’ U’) y (L’U’ L U)
second case
in this case if the top of edge's center in the left
alogrthim: U’ (L’ U’ L U) y’ (R U R’ U’)
finally you got it and made third step

step four

making yellow cross

there is 3 cases in this step which solved by 2 alogrthims only
first case
in this case you have 0 edges around yellow center
alogrthim: F (R U R’ U’) F’ F (U R U’ R’) F’
second case
in this case you have 2 edges around yellow center like right angle
alogrthim: F (U R U’ R’) F’
third case
in this case you have 2 edges around yellow center like horizontal bar
alogrthim: F (R U R’ U’) F’
finally you got it and made fourth step
